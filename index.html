About me

Hello, My name is Andrea Baker! I am a HBCU Graduate from the illustrious Florida Memorial University. I graduated with my BA in Communications with a focus in Broadcast. I love technology, activism, media and arts such as Videography and literature. On my portfolio site you will see a collection of these things as well be linked to some of my projects.

I think that videography, photography and writing are very essential to both daily life and for entertainment purposes. To be able to capture an image is to capture a moment. I also think it essential to be able to write your own and control a narrative!

I believe it is wonderful to be well-rounded and as you continue browsing my site... I hope you see just a few of the many hobbies I do have.
